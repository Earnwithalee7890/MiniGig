# Support Policy 🤝

Thank you for using **MiniGig**! We are committed to providing the best support for our users and contributors on the Celo network.

## 🔍 How to Get Help

Before reaching out, please check the following resources:

1. **[README.md](README.md)**: Contains installation instructions and basic usage.
2. **[FAQ](docs/FAQ.md)**: Common questions and troubleshooting steps.
3. **[GitHub Issues](https://github.com/Earnwithalee7890/MiniGig/issues)**: Search for existing issues or report a new one.

## 💬 Contact Us

If you can't find the answer you're looking for, feel free to reach out through the following channels:

- **Discord**: Join our community at [MiniGig Discord](https://discord.gg/minigig).
- **Twitter/X**: Follow us and send a DM to [@MiniGigCelo](https://twitter.com/MiniGigCelo).
- **Email**: Send your inquiries to [support@minigig.celo](mailto:support@minigig.celo).

## 🐛 Reporting Bugs

When reporting a bug, please include:
- A clear description of the issue.
- Steps to reproduce the bug.
- Environment details (Browser, Wallet, OS).
- Screenshots or console logs if applicable.

## 💡 Feature Requests

We love hearing your ideas! Please use the GitHub Issues "Feature Request" template to suggest new features or improvements.
